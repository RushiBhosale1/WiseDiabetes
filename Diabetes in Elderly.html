Age-Appropriate Diabetes Management

1. Root Cause Evaluation
We adapt our treatment to the patient’s age, lifestyle, comorbidities, and physical capacity. The goal is gentle sugar control without hypoglycemia or unnecessary medicine load.

2. Fall & Foot Complication Prevention
Older patients are more prone to foot ulcers, nerve damage, and falls. We provide:
-Foot nerve testing and circulation checks
-Customized MCR footwear
-Balance improvement and fall prevention tips


3. Minimal Medication Strategy
As polypharmacy (multiple medications) can harm seniors, we focus on:
-Safely reducing unnecessary diabetes drugs
-Using newer-generation medicines with fewer side effects
-Supporting sugar control through diet and activity


4. Diet & Nutrition Plans for Seniors
Our dietitian provides customized diets that are:
Easy to chew and digest
Rich in protein and fiber
Balanced to avoid low sugars
We even support caregivers in meal planning for seniors with dementia or swallowing issues.


5. Screening for Silent Complications
Many elderly patients develop:
-Silent heart disease
-Fatty liver
-Diabetic kidney damage
We perform routine screening via our Advanced Diabetes Lab, ABPM, Fundoscopy, and Fibroscan to detect early and act timely.


6. Family Involvement & Education
We encourage family support through:
Video consults for distant family members
Written care summaries after each visit
Clear instructions on medications and red flags
